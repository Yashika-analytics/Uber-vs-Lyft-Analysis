# 🚗 Uber vs Lyft - Boston Ride Analysis | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
Competitive analysis of 693,000+ Uber and Lyft rides across 
Boston in 2018, comparing pricing, market share and demand patterns 
between two of the world's biggest ride sharing platforms.

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
1. Which platform holds more market share?
2. Which platform is more expensive on average?
3. Which ride types have the biggest price gap?
4. Which days of the week see the highest demand?
5. What time of day do rides peak?

## 💡 Key Insights
- 🔵 **Uber leads** market share at 51.82% vs Lyft's 48.18%
- 💰 **Lyft charges $1.56 more** on average despite lower market share
- 🚘 **Lux Black XL** is Lyft's most expensive ride type
- 📅 **Monday and Tuesday** are the busiest days for both platforms
- 🌙 **Demand peaks after 10PM** for both platforms

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop**
- **DAX** — calculated columns extracting Day and Hour from raw timestamps
- **Dataset:** Uber & Lyft Boston Rides from Kaggle

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- ✅ Lyft vs Uber toggle filter
- ✅ Market share donut chart
- ✅ Average price by ride type comparison
- ✅ Rides by day of week
